Dashboard

WELCOME

Our mission was to provide an interactive dashboard visualizing US Presidential Elections from 2000 - 2016. We have visualized results for each state and county, indicating the winning party by color for the participating territories. When you hoover over a state, the results for each party are displayed along with the details of their win or loss by percentage. When you click on a state, you will be provided a summary along with a detailed view of the results for each county. The data was pulled from Harvard's Dataverse Repository and the Exploratory websites.
